<span data-ttu-id="7466e-105">HoloLens 设备的其中一项易管理的功能是使用 [Settings/PageVisibilityList 策略](https://docs.microsoft.com/windows/client-management/mdm/policy-csp-settings#settings-pagevisibilitylist)限制“设置”应用中显示的页面。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7466e-105">One of the manageable features for HoloLens devices is using the [Settings/PageVisibilityList policy](https://docs.microsoft.com/windows/client-management/mdm/policy-csp-settings#settings-pagevisibilitylist) to restrict the pages seen within the Settings app.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="7466e-106">PageVisibilityList 是一项策略，它允许 IT 管理员阻止查看或访问“系统设置”应用中的特定页面，或者对除指定页面之外的所有页面执行此操作。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7466e-106">PageVisibilityList is a policy that allows IT Admins to either prevent specific pages in the System Settings app from being visible or accessible, or to do so for all pages except those specified.</span></span>
