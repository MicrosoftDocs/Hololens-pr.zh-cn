<span data-ttu-id="41f02-169">在 HoloLens 2 中，目视位置支持准确的全息图定位、舒适的查看体验和改进的显示质量。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="41f02-169">In HoloLens 2, eye positions enable accurate hologram positioning, comfortable viewing experience and improved display quality.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="41f02-170">眼位置将作为眼睛跟踪结果的一部分进行计算。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="41f02-170">Eye positions are computed as part of the eye tracking result.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="41f02-171">但是，这要求每个用户都可以通过目视跟踪校准，即使体验不需要目视的注视输入也是如此。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="41f02-171">However, this requires each user to go through eye tracking calibration, even when the experience does not require eye gaze input.</span></span>
